WHAT ARE THERMAL IMAGING INSPECTIONS?

Thermal imaging, also known as infrared thermography, is a valuable predictive maintenance method that can monitor the thermal signatures of machinery, electrical systems, and building infrastructure without interfering with regular operations.

In Toronto, regular thermographic inspections can be a highly effective way to optimize equipment and systems while preventing costly and inconvenient equipment failures.
